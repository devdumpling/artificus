# The castle

![](https://cdn.discordapp.com/attachments/1018201519100792943/1100038646104604752/Devlathar_an_enormous_gothic_castle_hidden_in_thick_mist_and_fo_e6162ae2-5f75-4101-b252-b92ef12a3fea.png)

The castle, an enormous gothic castle mysteriously hidden in the mist passages of Old Pinemont, is the headquarters of the [Legion of Death](/characters/legion-of-death). It does not seem to have a formal name, and is just called "the castle" by the residents.
